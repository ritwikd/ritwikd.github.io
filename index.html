CMU Paper
Attached is an academic paper I wrote based on my research on academic social networks. I did my research this past summer (2015) with Professor Radu Marculescu and his graduate students in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at CMU. I was interested in big data before, and this research gave me big insight into efficient extraction, sensible representation, and meaningful analysis of large electronic data sets. Review of existing literature and past work also taught me about network science and use of graph theory in predictive analysis. The analysis methodology we proposed in the paper can handle a complex set of variables, hence can be extended to analyze different types of complex social networks. In particular, I believe our results can be used to guide and optimize policies regarding the rules of engagement in different academic events. The project was not only a great learning experience, I also made new friends in Professor Marculescu's graduate student group.
